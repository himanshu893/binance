Validation Rules

The form follows the current Python validation: side is BUY or SELL, type is MARKET, LIMIT, or STOP, quantity must be greater than zero, LIMIT and STOP require price and time in force, and STOP also requires stop price.

Without a running backend, submissions are saved to browser local storage as local history records.
